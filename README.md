# HostingSift Community Providers

A community-driven list of hosting providers for [HostingSift](https://hostingsift.com) — a hosting comparison platform with real pricing data, plan details, and side-by-side comparisons.

We currently track **45 providers** and **1224 plans**, but there are many great hosting companies we haven't covered yet. That's where you come in!

## Found wrong data?

All provider data is updated weekly. Already tracking a provider that has incorrect pricing or missing plans? [Open an issue](../../issues/new) and let us know — corrections are just as valuable as new suggestions!

## What we're looking for

We're especially interested in:

- **Niche providers** — game servers, managed WordPress, dev-focused VPS, PaaS
- **Regional providers** — great in specific markets (EU, Asia, LATAM, Africa)
- **Price/performance gems** — providers that don't get talked about much but deliver great value
- **Specialized hosting** — GPU, high-memory, storage-optimized, edge hosting

## What happens after you suggest?

1. We review the suggestion
2. Research the provider's plans and pricing
3. Build a scraper to track their data automatically
4. Add them to [hostingsift.com](https://hostingsift.com) with full plan details and comparisons
